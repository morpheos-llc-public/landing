APPROACH

  • For better or worse, AI is now embedding into all industries. Working knowledge about AI is quickly becoming a necessity in the workplace.

  • Morpheos offers AI literacy and implementation coaching for professionals and teams. We teach clients about the origins and state-of-the-art for AI models, key features and limitations, and how to integrate in a world where AI usage is rapidly becoming unavoidable.

  • We deploy philosophical and hands-on methods to teach folks how to approach AI for novel and unconventional purposes beyond basic email drafting and memo writing.

  • Overview: together we review key background about AI, assess your workflow and pain points, share favorite tools, and then brainstorm ways to enable you to perform feats that were previously impractical or impossible.

    • It all starts with deep listening. At Morpheos, we listen and assess your goals, workflows, and pain points. We then tailor our training to your specific needs.
