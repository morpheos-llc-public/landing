APPROACH

  • As AI is embedding into all industries, working knowledge about AI is now becoming a necessity in the workplace.

  • Morpheos offers AI adoption and implementation coaching for individuals and teams. We teach clients about the origins and state-of-the-art for AI models, key features and limitations, and how to integrate in a world where AI usage is rapidly becoming unavoidable.

  • In 90 minute coaching sessions, we deploy philosophical and hands-on methods to teach clients how to approach AI for novel and unconventional purposes beyond basic email drafting and memo writing.

  • Session Overview: Together we review key background about AI, assess your workflow and pain points, share favorite tools, and then brainstorm ways to enable you to perform feats that were previously impractical or impossible.

    ABOUT

    Brandon Hogstad, PhD is an AI-implementation coach who helps executives and product teams put generative AI to work. Drawing on a deep research background, Brandon excels at distilling complex science and technology into clear, practical playbooks that drive immediate results.

    Through his firm Morpheos Innovation, Brandon also serves as a consultant and entrepreneur-in-residence, guiding venture-backed startups and academic spin-outs across biotech, health-tech, and deep-tech. He specializes in strategic commercialization, alliance management, and scientific due diligence—bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and market-ready products.

    Previously, he built drug-discovery accelerator programs at NYU Langone Health, forging collaborations with Sanofi and Pfizer (contributing research that informed Paxlovid). He later led AI-driven immunotherapy initiatives at Immunai, harnessing machine learning and single-cell genomics for precision medicine.

    Brandon earned his PhD in Biomedical Sciences from the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai; his work has appeared in Nature and the Journal of Experimental Medicine. Outside of consulting and coaching, he co-hosts the dream-interpretation podcast Let's Talk About Dreams and releases music with his brother as The Hogstad Brothers.
